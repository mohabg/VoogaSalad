This package describes how collisions are handled in the game. The CollisionChecker uses javafx's intersects method to make sure a collision occurred, and if a collision occurs, reflection is used to determine what behavior the sprites who are colliding should experience, then the handleCollision from the respective class is called. There are many types of collisions, including DamageCollision(takes health away from a colliding sprite), PowerUpCollision(gives a power up to a colliding sprite), ExplosionCollision(causes the sprite in question to explode), etc. The names are fairly self explanatory.
